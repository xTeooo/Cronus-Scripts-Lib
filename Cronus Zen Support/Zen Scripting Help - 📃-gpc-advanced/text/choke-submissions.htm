UFC 4 Choke Submissions Tips & tricks

Learn how to improve your skills in UFC 4

Back to Tips & Tricks Hub

Choke Submission Offense

Use ^LS^ to move your wedge and chase the opponent's wedge. The longer your wedge covers the opponent's and the more they overlap, the more your Submission meter grows.

Inputs:

^LS^

Choke Submission Defense

Use ^LS^ to move your wedge around the circle to move away from the attacker. Moving too quickly or side to side will cause your wedge to grow. Using controlled movement will keep your wedge small.

Inputs:

^LS^

Damage Effects

If the attacker achieves a lot of progress in a choke, the defender's stamina will be drained.
