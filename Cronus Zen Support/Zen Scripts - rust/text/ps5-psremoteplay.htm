PS REMOTE PLAY

Cronus Zen supports the official Sony PS REMOTE PLAY apps for Windows, MacOS, Android & Apple iOS! Connect any supported PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo controller to a PS5, all with full MOD & GPC scripting capabilities.

👉 This page includes the following important information for PlayStation 5 gaming with the PS REMOTE PLAY method:

  • ﻿﻿﻿Pro & Cons
  • ﻿﻿﻿PS REMOTE PLAY Video Demo
  • ﻿Requirements & Restrictions

👉 Try the official, or open-source apps (you may find one working better than the other):

  • ﻿﻿Sony Official Apps ﻿
  • ﻿﻿﻿﻿Chiaki Open-Source Apps ﻿

PROS & CONS

🟢 PS REMOTE PLAY PROS

﻿

  • Almost all PS5/PS4 games work
  • Does not require HORI or PS5 Dongle﻿﻿
  • DUALSENSE Adaptive Trigger (USB)
  • DUALSENSE Haptic Feedback (USB)
  • Rumble support with most games (USB)
  • DUALSENSE/DS4 SPEAKER (USB)
  • Headset Audio support (USB)

🔴 PS REMOTE PLAY CONS

﻿

  • Fast, wired internet required
  • PC, Mac, iOS or Android device required
  • USB-C OTG cable required for Android, iOS
  • May experience latency over Wi-Fi
  • Some PS5 games may not work
  • Some PS4 games may not work
  • Share Screen / Share Play does not work

RESTRICTIONS

  • Some games don't support PS REMOTE PLAY.
    • Games that require peripherals such as PlayStationVR or PlayStation Camera aren’t compatible with PS REMOTE PLAY.
    • Some PS5 and PS4 games may not play (let us know if you find one).
  • You can't use PS REMOTE PLAY on your PS5 console in the following situations:
    • When you're using Share Screen, or Share Play, or broadcasting your gameplay.
    • When you're playing a Blu-ray Disc, or DVD.
    • When you're using PlayStationVR in VR mode.

One of the PS Remote Play methods may work great for somebody else, but it doesn't necessarily mean you will get the same results, so we recommend that you try both methods to see which works best for your setup.
