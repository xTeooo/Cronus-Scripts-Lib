RESTRICTIONS

  • Some games don't support PS REMOTE PLAY.
    • Games that require peripherals such as PlayStationVR or PlayStation Camera arenâ€™t compatible with PS REMOTE PLAY.
    • Some PS5 and PS4 games may not play (let us know if you find one).
  • You can't use PS REMOTE PLAY on your PS5 console in the following situations:
    • When you're using Share Screen, or Share Play, or broadcasting your gameplay.
    • When you're playing a Blu-ray Disc, or DVD.
