These mods are not accessible through the GamePack Configuration Interface (GCI), these mods are only accessible in the Zen OLED Menu System.

Ⓜ️ QuickScope

You will perform a QuickScope with one Button click. On Fire is when you hit the Fire button (Default: RT/R2) once. On ADS is when you hit the ADS button (Default: LT/L2) once. The fire and release time can be adjusted to your personal preference in the GamePack menu.

Ⓜ️ Shortcut Keys: Elite Paddles or Zen PS4 Back Button Attachment

Save time and quickly change the active weapon profile in use simply by holding either activator 1 or activator 2 and pressing a face button, bumper, trigger, click LS / L3 or RS / R3. While enabled hold View / Touchpad + LS / L3 to temporarily disable Shortcut keys for Elite Paddles or the Zen Back Button Attachment. When toggling this mod using the jump toggle you will feel a slight rumble. This will allow you to use your paddles as you normally would. This mod must be first enabled in the menu of the GamePack for the toggle to function correctly. When you would like to use the mod again simply re-enable it and you are good to go!
